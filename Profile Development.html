Profile Development



Most top Global Universities want a Diverse class along with relevant merit. The merit criteria are widely spread – between Academics, Extra Curricular activities, Unique Skills and/or Life experiences, Standardized tests, Displayed Values through Life Experiences; to Roles of responsibility in Community, School and Society.

We help our students to build a relevant profile along with articulation and presentation of that profile to relevant Universities.


Everyone has and continues to create a Digital Footprint through Digital activities.

We help review such Digital Footprint and provide guidance for creating and maintaining a ‘right’ footprint on all Digital platforms.


Many universities want to look at a student’s resume. This is one of many ways in which they try to know the student better. This also becomes a basis of interviews that some universities conduct before admitting students.

We help build a resume for our students to highlight their unique achievements, to effectively articulate what students bring to the relevant university (when admitted) and hence what they can contribute to the university.
