Leadership



Rupali Deolalikar-Dehade

Ara is manifestation of Founder and Managing Director Rupali Deolalikar-Dehade's passion. Her passion to help students achieve their full potential, to build a strong foundation to be ideal citizens of our world and to lead them to Universities/Colleges which will help achieve both these objectives.

It is this passion of Rupali that has made Ara one of India's fastest growing Admissions Consulting and Solutions providers. Ara's domain expertise is an outcome of Rupali's extensive and detailed research.

Given her continuously deepening passion for adding value to our future generations, Rupali has been associated with Education for almost two decades. She has been teaching various subjects in Architecture at SNDT (Juhu, Mumbai), INIFD (Andheri, Mumbai), KRVIA (Juhu, Mumbai), Sushant School of Art & Architecture and Sushant School of Design (Gurgaon). She has a Bachelor's Degree in Architecture from M S University of Baroda and has completed Master's Degree in Urban Planning from Institute of Town Planners; India's premier Town Planning body.

Rupali has been a practicing Architect for over 25 years having worked on Commercial and non-Commercial projects across Gujarat, Maharashtra, Delhi-NCR and Rajasthan.
Ashish Dehade

A big believer in "...creating stories that we can tell our grand-children with pride...", that is, do more to transform our world, Ashish is continuously looking for new opportunities to positively impact maximum number of lives. He insists that such large impact on lives can happen through focus on Healthcare and Education.

Ashish has been passionate about 'how' along with the 'what'... it is not just about 'what' we deliver, it is also about 'how' we do what we do - that is, with respect and without compromising Values, Principles, Culture and Ethics.

As a Co-Founder, Ashish focuses on the strategic direction and overall performance of Ara - and its long term growth agenda. He is involved in Mentoring and Coaching of students through the end-to-end Admissions process.

Previously, Ashish has been accountable for Global Strategic Planning, Sales & Marketing and Operations for large enterprises, across North and South America, Europe and Asia Pacific. He has held positions as Global Business Head and Global Head of Strategic Transformation at UnitedHealth Care Operations, Optum Global Solutions and Cognizant Technology Solutions; and Group COO and Managing Director (West Asia and ASEAN) for First Advantage (world's largest Background Screening Company). He has also held senior positions at Accenture and CEAT.
