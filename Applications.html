Applications



We provide end-to-end support for filling online applications of all selected Universities. This includes presenting the student in a holistic way, prioritizing presentation of specific attributes, personality traits and achievements of the student; articulating such achievements along with extra curricular activities effectively; and coaching support to write essays.


Many universities want to meet/speak with students before admitting them. This is one more way in which they seek to know students better.

We help our students to prepare for such interviews through multiple mentoring sessions and mock interviews.


Each top global University wants to know a student better and his/her life’s journey along with learnings therefrom; before admitting him/her.

The essays that these universities want students to write as part of their application have proven to be an effective method to know what motivates a student and/or what has inspired a student and/or what has made the student what he/she is. That is, such essays are a window for the universities to peep into a student’s life, personality and character. Its their way to hear a student’s story – to know the student as well as possible. These essays very often become the final decision point for most universities to accept or reject an application.

We help our students to brain storm and ideate on what they can present in their essays. Our focus is to enable our students to articulate their thoughts; that is, tell their story better and more effectively.
