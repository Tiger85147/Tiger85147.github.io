Priyanka Kaushik-Malik
Partner, Design & Architecture
A Master of Architecture from School of Planning and Architecture (SPA, Del-hi), Priyanka is a specialist in Industrial Design. She has been in Education and Counseling for almost 15 years, while continuing to teach Architecture and Design at SPA-Delhi; IIT-Delhi; Vaastukala Academy, Sushant School of Design and Amity University.

Having worked in Design teams of Usha Lexus and Essar Telecom Retail, Priyanka started ‘The Futurist’, an Education Counseling firm that specializes in supporting high school students aspiring to pursue Architecture and Design for Graduation and Post-Graduation globally.

At Ara, Priyanka leads the Design and Architecture vertical, solely focusing on students seeking admissions to global Universities for Design and Architecture.
